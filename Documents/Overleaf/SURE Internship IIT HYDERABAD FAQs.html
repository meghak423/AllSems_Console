FAQs on Summer Undergraduate Research Exposure Internship Programme

1. The given link is not working. What should I do?
 Ans: Please use the given link: https://iith.ac.in/research/SURE/

2. What is the cost of the SURE Internship?
 Ans: The SURE internship offered by IIT Hyderabad is completely free of cost. Please also refer to questions 3 and 4 below.

3. Are any internships paid for the students of SURE?
 Ans: Yes, successful interns will be paid an honorarium of ₹15,000 for the two-month duration. If a student opts for a 1.5-month internship, they will receive ₹10,000. For a one-month internship, the honorarium will be ₹7,500.

4. Does the ₹15,000 fellowship cover hostel fees?
 Ans: Hostel facilities will be provided free of cost. However, food charges will need to be borne by the selected student. Apart from the internship honorarium, no other benefits are provided.

5. Are there any medical facilities available on the IITH campus? Are these facilities extended to the interns?
 Ans: Basic medical facilities are available on campus. Outpatient services can be extended to the interns at nominal charges, as per IITH norms applicable for project staff. Interns will need to arrange their own medicines.

6. Is the SURE internship offered online or offline?
 Ans: This internship is offered only in offline mode. It is NOT available online or part-time.

7. My university classes will end in May, or exams will start from July 2025. Can I join for one month?
 Ans: Yes, you can refer to the relevant part in the application and opt for a one-month internship. However, in such a case, the internship will be paid for only one month.

8. I am currently in my 3rd year of Integrated M.Sc./Dual Degree. Am I eligible to apply?
 Ans: Yes, 3rd-year and 4th-year Integrated M.Sc./Dual Degree students are eligible to apply.

9. I am a B.Sc. 2nd or 3rd-year student. Am I eligible to apply?
 Ans: No, B.Sc. students are not eligible to apply.

10. I am studying B.Tech 2nd or 3rd year at IIT Hyderabad. Can I apply for the SURE Internship program?
 Ans: No, this programme is ONLY for non-IITH students.

11. In the SURE application form, there is a question: "Are you in the top 20% of your batch?" Does this refer to the complete BTech (2021-25) batch or only my branch (CSE)?
 Ans: The "batch" refers only to your respective branch in your institution.

12. I am in M.A./M.Sc. 2nd semester, but my 1st-semester result is not out. Am I eligible?
 Ans: You are not eligible. You may apply next year.

13. Could you provide more information on the department and topics covered in the internship?
 Ans: Please refer to the details of the departments from the dropdown menu in the application. Faculty names and areas of interest are also available.

14. I am in my 2nd year of BSc in triple majors. Will my application be considered as I am in the top 10% of my batch?
 Ans: No, B.Sc. students are not eligible.

15. Though I am a 2nd-year B.Tech student, my college has not completed even the 1st semester. How can I apply?
 Ans: You are not eligible. You may apply next year.

16. I am currently pursuing M.Sc. 2nd semester of 1st year and do not have my last semester marksheet as the results are not published. Am I eligible?
 Ans: You are not eligible. You may apply for next year's SURE Internship program.

17. I am a student of Electronics and Communications Engineering. Can I apply for the SURE Internship?
 Ans: The internship program is offered only in the streams mentioned in the application form.

18. I have completed my B.Tech/M.Tech/M.Sc. program. Am I eligible to apply?
 Ans: This program is only available to B.Tech, M.Tech, and M.Sc. pursuing students, as mentioned in the poster and application form.

19. Can I change my mentor after the internship has been allocated?
 Ans: No changes are allowed as you have already opted for a specific guide/mentor while applying for the internship.

20. I have some queries that are not listed above. Whom should I contact?
 Ans: If your query is not listed above, please send an email to src.sure@iith.ac.in. The concerned staff will respond to your email. Please note that no response will be provided if the query has already been answered above.
